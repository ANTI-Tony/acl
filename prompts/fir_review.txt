### Input
**Tuple A (Original)**:
- Instruction: {instruction_a}
- Output: {output_a}

**Tuple B (Refined)**:
- Instruction: {instruction_b}
- Output: {output_b}

### Evaluation Criteria
1. Problem clarity and completeness
2. Solution correctness (step-by-step verification)
3. Reasoning quality and completeness
4. Overall training value for math reasoning

### Response Format
```json
{
  "winner": "A" or "B",
  "reason": "brief explanation of why the winner is better",
  "feedback_if_A_wins": "if A wins, provide feedback on why the refinement failed and how to improve next iteration"
}
```
